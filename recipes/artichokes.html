Artichokes


Description

So basically you will cut the tail of the artichoke and then smash the flower against a hard, flat surface so it opens, wash them thoroughly and then place it on an oven tray, put some olive oil on top, salt and bacon inside the flower of the artichoke. Bake for at least an hour, until the core is soft.

Ingredients

  • Artichokes
  • Oil
  • Bacon
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the tail of the artichoke
  2. Smash the flower against a hard, flat surface so it opens
  3. Wash them thoroughly
  4. Place it on an oven tray
  5. Put some olive oil on top, salt and bacon inside the flower of the artichoke
  6. Bake for at least an hour, until the core is soft
